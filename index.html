A message from the Principal — Enthusiastic & Proud

Dr. P. Thangavel, ME, MBA, PhD
Principal
Gobi — Since 2008

I am extremely happy to welcome you to Shree Venkateshwara Hi-Tech Engineering College, Gobi. Started in 2008-09 with an objective to provide quality education and excellence in the ever-changing field of technical education. Technology is moving at a very fast pace — and we move with it.

The College has separate spacious buildings for each department with very well equipped undergraduate and postgraduate laboratories. Besides this, the college has a newly constructed computer laboratory, central library cum-reading room, sports facilities, modern gym, health center, career guidance cell and financial assistance cell for meritorious as well as financially poor and backward students. All the departments and the library are provided with internet facility.

The teaching staff of the college are well qualified with profound knowledge of their subjects and are devoted wholeheartedly to the well-being of the college and students. Most teachers are members or life members of academic and professional societies. To keep them abreast with the latest trends, they regularly attend and organize conferences, symposia, seminars and workshops. The departmental libraries of postgraduate departments have a good collection of books and journals.

Every year students from different classes secure places in the university merit lists. Many students are selected in various competitive examinations and thus add glory to the college. Success cannot be achieved without a peaceful atmosphere — we enjoy complete harmony between students, teachers, the principal and the management. All teaching and non-teaching staff contribute their bit in upgrading the college environment.

Why choose us?

  • Dedicated, experienced teaching staff
  • Regular workshops, seminars & conferences
  • Proven student achievements in university merit lists
  • Supportive, peaceful campus culture
